Frequently Asked Questions

+ What is Sancta & where is it located?

SANCTA is an exclusive boutique residentialproject located in the heart of La Paz, Baja California Sur. It offers a seamless blend of modern urban living and natural beauty, with a design inspired by the richness of the local environment. Its prime location in downtown La Paz provides residents with immediate access to the vibrant city life and the serenity of a peaceful retreat.

+ How many exclusive units does Sancta offer?

SANCTA features only 21 exclusive units, with 12 units remaining available for sale.

+ What types of living spaces are available and what are their sizes?

SANCTA offers a variety of floor plans designed to suit different lifestyles:

  • San Borjita: 1 bedroom, 1 bathroom, total area of 70.64 m².
  • San Ignacio: 2 bedrooms, 2 bathrooms (1 with walk-in closet), total area of 105.81 m²
  • San Ignacio Plata: 2 bedrooms, 2 bathrooms (1 with walk-in closet), total area of 110.74 m²
  • Mulegé: 2 bedrooms, 2 bathrooms (1 with walk-in closet), total area of 131.98 m² (includes large terraces).
  • Loreto: 2 bedrooms, 2 bathrooms, total area of 102.92 m².
  • San Borja: 2 bedrooms, 2 bathrooms, total area of 123.64 m² (with inspiring views).
+ What luxury amenities do residents enjoy?

Residents of SANCTA enjoy exclusive amenities such as a swimming pool with 360° views, a jacuzzi, hammocks, fire pits, gardens, and a relaxation lounge.

+ What are the benefits of choosing Sancta?
  • Proximity: Just a short distance from La Paz's culinary center and the malecón (boardwalk).
  • Design: Unique architecture that responds to the local climate, preserving natural elements like mature trees.
  • Comfort: Spacious layouts, elevators, and thoughtful features for modern living.
+ What is the purchase process and reservation deposit?

The purchase process includes a purchase agreement with custom contracts and flexible payment schedules, and full assistance for both national and international buyers at closing.

+ What is the payment plan?
  • Initial Deposit
  • Earnest Money Deposit: 30% of the Total Purchase Price (including Initial Deposit), paid at the signing of the Promise to Trust Agreement (15 calendar days after acceptance of offer).
  • Second Deposit: 25% of the Total Purchase Price, paid at the start of vertical construction.
  • Third Deposit: 25% of the Total Purchase Price, paid at the grey scale stage.
  • Final Deposit: 20% of the Total Purchase Price, paid upon delivery.
  • The offer price in USD will be registered in MXP at the official exchange rate on the day of the Deed.

+ How can I get more information or reserve a unit?
