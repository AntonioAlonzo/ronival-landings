Frequently Asked Questions

+ What is El Rincon and where is it located?

El Rincon is a residential development by Nopoló Hills, situated in Loreto, Baja California Sur. It offers homes and condos perfectly located near the enchanting bay and backed by majestic mountain scenery

+ Who is the developer of El Rincon and Cortes Coast?

The developer is Nopoló Hills. Ronival Real Estate is the sales agency
