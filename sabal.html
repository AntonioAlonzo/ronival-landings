Frequently Asked Questions

+ Where is Casa Sabal located?

Casa Sabal is located in the prestigious Fundadores community within Puerto Los Cabos, a gated neighborhood in San José del Cabo, Mexico.

+ How large is the property?

Casa Sabal offers a total of 6,595 sq. ft. of interior living space and 750 sq. ft. of outdoor terraces and decks.

+ What are the key features of Casa Sabal?

This modern home includes 4 spacious bedrooms with en-suite access to gardens and pool, 5 spa-inspired bathrooms, a private 3-car garage, a rooftop with ocean views, and indoor-outdoor living spaces designed for entertaining.

+ Does the property have ocean views?

Yes. Casa Sabal features panoramic ocean views from the rooftop and mountain views from the pool terrace.

+ What outdoor amenities are included?

The home includes a private pool, jacuzzi, fire pit, BBQ area, and beautifully landscaped gardens.

+ Is the kitchen equipped with appliances?

Yes. The home comes with premium kitchen and laundry appliances, offering convenience and top performance.

+ What amenities does the Fundadores community offer?

Owners have access to a private beachfront clubhouse with oceanfront dining and pools, a 27-hole golf course by Jack Nicklaus and Greg Norman, a full-service spa, gym, tennis courts, a yoga pavilion, and direct access to the Puerto Los Cabos Marina.

+ How close is Casa Sabal to downtown San José del Cabo?

It is just minutes away from the historic center of San José, where you’ll find gourmet dining, art galleries, and boutique shopping.

+ Is this home a good investment opportunity?

Yes. With its location, modern design, and world-class amenities, Casa Sabal is ideal as a primary residence, vacation home, or high-end rental property in one of Mexico’s most sought-after real estate markets.

+ How can I schedule a private viewing?

Contact us directly to arrange a private tour. We’ll be happy to show you why Casa Sabal is one of the finest homes available in Los Cabos.
