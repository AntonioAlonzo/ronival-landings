Frequently Asked Questions

+ Where is Torre Bivalvia located?

Torre Bivalvia is located in one of the most charming and highly sought-after neighborhoods in La Paz, Baja California Sur. It is just steps away from the city’s iconic Malecón and historic downtown, surrounded by restaurants, coffee shops, and art galleries.

+ Why is the location so special?

The project sits in the heart of La Paz’s Historic District, offering both cultural richness and convenience. Everything is within walking distance—the waterfront, downtown, cultural hubs, and schools. This unique location not only enhances lifestyle but also maximizes rental appeal.

+ What types of residences are available?

Torre Bivalvia offers boutique-style residences with two bedrooms, two and a half bathrooms, and 1,314 sq.ft. of living space. Each unit also includes a laundry and storage area. The design blends contemporary Mexican and coastal contemporary styles, creating a modern yet warm atmosphere.

+ What amenities does Bivalvia provide?

Residents enjoy a rooftop lifestyle with panoramic bay views. Amenities include:

  • An infinity pool overlooking La Paz Bay
  • Fire pit and lounge area
  • Grill and bar for entertaining
  • Visitor parking
  • Shared lounge spaces designed for comfort and community
+ How many residences are available?

This is a boutique development with limited availability. Only four units remain, making it an exclusive opportunity for buyers and investors.

+ What is the lifestyle like in La Paz?

La Paz is known as the “Pearl of the Sea of Cortez.” It offers a unique balance of serenity and vibrancy. The Malecón is the city’s cultural hub, perfect for evening walks, art events, and dining. World-class beaches like Balandra and Tecolote are minutes away, making it ideal for outdoor lovers and those seeking an authentic yet modern coastal lifestyle.

+ How strong is the real estate market in La Paz?

La Paz is one of the strongest-performing real estate markets in Mexico. Baja California Sur recorded 17.6% annual appreciation, the highest in the country. La Paz leads in capital gains, outperforming even established resort cities.

*The information contained in this presentation is based on public sources and specialized reports available as of the end of 2024

+ Is Bivalvia a good investment for rentals?

Yes. La Paz has become a hot market for vacation rentals. Occupancy rates exceed 70%, and revenues have grown 75% year-over-year, with nightly rates rising 25%. Thanks to its walkable location in Esterito, Bivalvia offers excellent Airbnb and rental income potential.

+ Who is the developer?

Torre Bivalvia is developed by Altamar Habitat, a Mexican company with a strong presence in Baja California Sur. Altamar specializes in luxury residential projects and is known for its architectural quality, modern design, and sustainable developments. Headquartered in La Paz, Altamar offers personalized attention to investors and homeowners.

+ What makes Bivalvia different from other projects in La Paz?

Unlike larger developments, Bivalvia offers boutique exclusivity, with only a handful of units. Its rooftop lifestyle, prime location in Esterito, and high appreciation potential make it a rare blend of cultural authenticity, comfort, and investment opportunity.
